EXAMINATION INSTRUCTIONS

PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING CAREFULLY

  • No Refresh Policy: Do not refresh the page during the exam. Refreshing will submit your exam automatically and you won't be able to resume.
  • Negative Marking: For every incorrect answer, 0.25 marks will be deducted. Unanswered questions will not be penalized.
  • Answer All Questions: You must attempt all questions before submitting. The submit button will only activate after all questions are answered.
  • Time Limit: The exam has a strict time limit of 60 minutes. A timer will be displayed at the top of the screen. The exam will auto-submit when time expires.
  • No Switching Tabs: The system monitors tab switching. More than 3 attempts to switch tabs will result in automatic submission of your exam.
  • Full Screen Mode: The exam must be taken in full screen mode. You will be prompted to enter full screen when the exam begins.
  • Answer Selection: Carefully select your answers. Once moved to the next question, you cannot go back to previous questions.
  • No External Calculators: Use only the built-in calculator provided in the exam interface. External calculators are not permitted.
  • No Reference Materials: You are not allowed to use textbooks, notes, or any other reference materials during the exam.
  • Webcam Monitoring: Your webcam must be turned on throughout the exam for proctoring purposes. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
